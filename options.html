ABOUT

This extension filters DW pages such that the only comments that show up are the ones specifically whitelisted. Think of it as an advanced Ctrl + F.

CURRENTLY FILTERING TO

You can add multiple terms, just split them up with commas (,).

Text filtering rules

By default, terms will be searched for inclusively. For example:

  • fate will include fate/ series and stuff like resonance of fate.
  • ota will include phrases such as otaku or rotation.
  • persona will include phrases such as hassan of the hundred personas.
  • spider will include phrases such as into the spiderverse, spiderman, spider-man, etc.

To get the results you (probably) want, be more specific, and use wildcards (.*).

  • fate/.* will include fate/extra, fate/zero, fate/apocrypha, fate/ series, etc. but will not include resonance of fate (because it doesn't have the / anyway).
  • persona .* will include persona 2: innocent sin, persona 3, persona 3 portable, persona 4 arena, etc. but will not include hassan of the hundred personas.
  • tales of .* will include tales of berseria, tales of vesperia, tales of the abyss, etc. but not the canterbury tales.
